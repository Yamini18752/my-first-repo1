The Student Portal will act as a centralized platform for students, where they can:

 Login/Signup – Secure authentication for each student.

 Profile Management – View and update personal details.

 Courses & Subjects – Access course details, materials, and assignments.

 Attendance Tracking – Check attendance records.

 Grades & Results – View marks, progress, and performance reports.

 Notices & Announcements – Get important updates from the college/university.

 Feedback/Queries – Students can submit queries or feedback online
